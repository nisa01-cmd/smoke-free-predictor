About Smoke-Free Predictor

Learn about our AI-powered smoking cessation prediction system

What is Smoke-Free Predictor?

Smoke-Free Predictor is an advanced machine learning application designed to analyze various personal and behavioral factors to predict the likelihood of successful smoking cessation. Our AI model helps individuals and healthcare providers make informed decisions about smoking cessation strategies.

How it Works

Data Collection

We analyze key factors including demographics, lifestyle habits, stress levels, support systems, and motivation scores.

AI Processing

Our Random Forest algorithm processes the input data to identify patterns associated with successful smoking cessation.

Key Features

  • Individual Predictions: Get personalized success probability scores
  • Batch Processing: Analyze multiple cases simultaneously
  • REST API: Integrate predictions into your applications
  • Detailed Insights: Receive actionable recommendations
  • Privacy-Focused: No permanent data storage

Applications

Healthcare

Clinical decision support for smoking cessation programs

Research

Academic research on smoking cessation factors and outcomes

Personal Use

Individual assessment and self-improvement planning
